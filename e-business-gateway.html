Basic Facts to Understand Goal
  1. India has a high scarcity of natural resources for its population, including agricultural land, freshwater for irrigation, and forest area for healthy air.
  2. There is high economic inequality in India—80% of the population holds less than 3% of the wealth.
  3. High unemployment— a large portion of the population is unemployed or earns less than ₹10,000 per month.
  4. 60-70% of the population lives in rural or small towns, with agriculture and related activities as their primary source of income.
  5. The average income of an agricultural household is between ₹5,000 to ₹10,000 per month.
Needs / Solution / Goal

To increase the income of the rural population and decrease migration to cities, which are already over populated and over polluted, we need an e-business gateway to calculate and control:

  • Food demand
  • Agricultural production
  • Sale of agricultural produce
  • Transport
  • Export of produce

To minimize economic inequality, we need small, independent businesses.

a large portion of India's population is lower middle class or poor. India doesn’t just need a place to buy things; India needs a place to sell things. India needs C2C & C2B e-commerce to connect the primary sector:

  • Agriculture
  • Fishing
  • Livestock farming
  • Food supply
  • Solo trucking transport
  • Exporters

An e-business gateway is essential to facilitate the sale and export of agricultural, fishing, and livestock goods via solo open trucking and cold trucking.

We need an e-business gateway to connect and transact in the primary sector—agriculture, fishing, livestock farming, food supply, transport, and export.
